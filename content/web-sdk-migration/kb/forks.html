Five decisions, made and written down before you touch a setting. Making them explicit is what separates a six-week migration from a six-month one.

1 · Data homexdm vs data vs hybrid, hybrid, weighted to xdm
2 · Report suitevalidation suite first; real RSID only at cutover
3 · Profileleave OFF; enable later, deliberately
4 · Data layerkeep and map your existing layer
5 · Library loadTags extension + first-party CNAME edge domain

Alongside these sits the rollout approach: parallel-validate, then phase page-by-page (the hybrid). A big-bang cutover of a large estate concentrates every risk into one irreversible moment and is best avoided.

For your migration: the output is a one-page "migration profile" your whole inventory and plan refer back to. Decisions deferred here resurface as rework later.
